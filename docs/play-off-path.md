FIFA World Cup 2026 – Playoff Stage Structure (English)



This document describes the official knockout stage format for the FIFA World Cup 2026. The exact match pairings are not yet published by FIFA, but the structure and progression rules are fully defined.



1\. Overview



The 2026 World Cup features 48 teams in 12 groups (A–L). After the group stage, 32 teams advance to the knockout phase:



12 group winners



12 group runners‑up



8 best third‑placed teams



These 32 teams enter the Round of 32, which begins the playoff stage.



2\. Round of 32 (1/16 Finals)



The Round of 32 consists of 16 matches.



FIFA uses a predefined pairing system based on:



Group winners vs. third‑placed teams



Group winners vs. runners‑up



Runners‑up vs. runners‑up



Third‑placed teams vs. group winners



The exact bracket depends on which 8 third‑placed teams qualify.



3\. Round of 16



The winners from the Round of 32 advance to the Round of 16.



The bracket is fixed:



Winner Match 1 vs Winner Match 2



Winner Match 3 vs Winner Match 4



Winner Match 5 vs Winner Match 6



Winner Match 7 vs Winner Match 8



Winner Match 9 vs Winner Match 10



Winner Match 11 vs Winner Match 12



Winner Match 13 vs Winner Match 14



Winner Match 15 vs Winner Match 16



4\. Quarterfinals



The Round of 16 winners advance to the quarterfinals.



QF1: Winner R16 Match 1 vs Winner R16 Match 2



QF2: Winner R16 Match 3 vs Winner R16 Match 4



QF3: Winner R16 Match 5 vs Winner R16 Match 6



QF4: Winner R16 Match 7 vs Winner R16 Match 8



5\. Semifinals



The quarterfinal winners advance to the semifinals.



SF1: Winner QF1 vs Winner QF2



SF2: Winner QF3 vs Winner QF4



6\. Final \& Third‑Place Match



Final: Winner SF1 vs Winner SF2



Third‑Place Match: Loser SF1 vs Loser SF2



7\. Notes



The exact match schedule (dates, stadiums, pairings) will be published by FIFA after the group stage.



This document provides the official structure, which is stable and can be used to prepare backend logic, bracket generation, and UI components.



8\. Summary



The 2026 World Cup playoff stage follows a clear and predictable structure:



Round of 32 – 32 teams



Round of 16 – 16 teams



Quarterfinals – 8 teams



Semifinals – 4 teams



Final + Third‑Place Match – 2 + 2 teams



This format supports the expanded 48‑team tournament and ensures a balanced knockout progression.
